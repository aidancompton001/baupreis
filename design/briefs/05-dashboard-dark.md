# BauPreis AI Dashboard — Infographic (Dark Tech Style)

## Creative Brief for Luma Labs

### Topic & Scope

A stylized dashboard showcase infographic — NOT a screenshot, but an idealized, beautiful representation of what users see inside BauPreis AI. Shows the key dashboard elements: price charts, material cards, AI forecasts, alerts, and BauPreis Index. The goal is to make trial users excited and existing users proud of the tool they're using. Think "Apple product page" level of polish for a B2B dashboard.

### One Big Takeaway

Everything you need to control construction material costs — in one intelligent dashboard. Real-time prices, AI forecasts, smart alerts, and market indices, all designed for German construction professionals.

### Target Audience

Trial users exploring the platform and existing customers. They already know what BauPreis AI is — this infographic should make them feel "wow, this is a serious tool" and reinforce their decision to use/subscribe.

### Narrative Flow

A centered dashboard showcase with callout annotations:

1. **HERO DASHBOARD** — Large, stylized dashboard view in the center. Dark theme matching the actual product. Shows:
   - Navigation sidebar (left) with German menu items
   - Main area with a large price trend line chart (Stahlpreis 90 Tage)
   - Smaller cards below: material price tiles with green/red indicators

2. **CALLOUT: AI FORECAST** — Floating annotation card pointing to a forecast area: "KI-Prognose: Stahlpreis steigt 4.2% in 30 Tagen" with a confidence indicator

3. **CALLOUT: SMART ALERTS** — Floating annotation card: "Preisalarm: Kupfer überschreitet EUR 8,500/t" with a bell icon and push notification visual

4. **CALLOUT: BAUPREIS INDEX** — Floating annotation card pointing to an index gauge: "BauPreis Index: 127.3 — Markt steigend" with a semicircular gauge graphic

5. **BOTTOM STRIP** — Three plan badges: "Basis EUR 49 | Pro EUR 149 | Team EUR 299" with "Jetzt testen" CTA

### Key Stats to Include

- 16 materials tracked across the dashboard
- AI forecasts with 30/60/90 day horizons
- Real-time alerts via Email, Dashboard, Telegram
- BauPreis Index — proprietary market health indicator
- 52 pages, 46 API endpoints powering the experience

### Visual Style

Premium dark UI showcase. Deep navy (#0A1528) as the dashboard background, steel blue (#4A5C7E) for sidebar and secondary panels. White (#FFFFFF) text and chart labels. Orange accent for alert badges and CTA buttons. Lime green (#BEDD72) for positive price indicators and "up" arrows. The dashboard itself should look like a real, polished product — not a wireframe. Floating annotation cards have a subtle frosted glass effect (glassmorphism with backdrop blur). Thin glowing borders on cards. Chart lines are smooth, glowing curves. Overall: a premium SaaS product showcase — like Stripe's product pages or Linear's marketing site.

### Layout Concept

16:9 horizontal. The dashboard takes up ~70% of the center area, shown at a very slight 3D perspective tilt (like Apple's MacBook product shots). Three floating callout cards are positioned around the dashboard at angles, connected by thin dotted lines to their respective dashboard elements. Bottom 15%: plan pricing strip on slightly lighter background. Top: "Ihr Dashboard. Ihre Kontrolle." headline. Overall feel: an Apple-quality product showcase for a B2B SaaS tool.

### Format

16:9 horizontal — website features section / product tour page

### Tone

Premium and aspirational. "This is the tool top construction companies use." Polished, modern, impressive — the visual equivalent of test-driving a luxury car. Makes the viewer want to log in immediately.
