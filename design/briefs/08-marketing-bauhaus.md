# BauPreis AI — Pain to Solution Marketing (Bauhaus Style)

## Creative Brief for Luma Labs

### Topic & Scope

A marketing conversion infographic in bold Bauhaus style that tells the "pain → solution → result" story through pure geometric visual language. Three geometric worlds collide: chaos (disordered shapes), order (BauPreis AI grid), and success (harmonious composition). This is a statement piece — it converts through visual impact and bold confidence, not through soft persuasion.

### One Big Takeaway

Stop losing money to material price surprises. BauPreis AI gives you the same real-time price intelligence that large corporations have — starting at just EUR 49/month.

### Target Audience

Cold leads at the decision stage: Geschaeftsfuehrer (CEOs), CFOs, Einkaufsleiter (procurement heads) of small-to-medium German construction companies. They respond to directness, visual strength, and clear value propositions.

### Narrative Flow

A three-panel geometric story:

**PANEL 1 — "OHNE" / WITHOUT (left third)**
- Visual: geometric CHAOS — scattered red triangles, tilted black squares, broken circles, overlapping shapes at random angles
- Represents: disorganized procurement, surprise costs, wasted time
- Large red X in center
- Bold text: "OHNE BAUPREIS AI"
- Stat in yellow: "40-60% MATERIALKOSTEN UNKONTROLLIERT" (Source: Hauptverband der Deutschen Bauindustrie)
- Background: white with red accents

**PANEL 2 — "MIT" / WITH (center third)**
- Visual: the BauPreis AI logo as a large organizing force — geometric shapes from Panel 1 are being pulled into a grid pattern, aligning themselves
- Represents: the platform bringing order to chaos
- Red gear icon processing scattered data into organized rows
- Bold text: "BAUPREIS AI"
- Background: black with red and yellow geometric elements arranging themselves

**PANEL 3 — "ERGEBNIS" / RESULT (right third)**
- Visual: perfect geometric HARMONY — all shapes aligned in a clean Bauhaus grid composition. Rectangles in golden ratio, circles perfectly centered, triangles pointing up (growth)
- Represents: controlled costs, saved time, confident decisions
- Large yellow checkmark in center
- Bold text: "MIT BAUPREIS AI"
- Stats: "FUNDIERTE ENTSCHEIDUNGEN" / "3H ZEITERSPARNIS/WOCHE"
- Background: yellow with black elements

**BOTTOM STRIP** — Red banner: "7 TAGE KOSTENLOS TESTEN — BAUPREIS.AIS152.COM"

### Key Stats to Include

- Material cost overruns affect majority of construction projects (Source: Hauptverband der Deutschen Bauindustrie)
- Average procurement manager spends 3+ hours/week on manual price research
- BauPreis AI enables data-driven procurement decisions through real-time price intelligence
- 7-day free trial, no credit card required
- Starting at EUR 49/month (Basis plan)

### Visual Style

Pure Bauhaus geometric storytelling. Red (#C1292E), black (#1A1A1A), golden yellow (#F5C518), white (#FFFFFF), terracotta (#BC8279). The three panels use the same geometric shapes but in three states: chaotic, transitioning, harmonious. No illustrations of people, no realistic objects — ONLY abstract geometric shapes telling the story. Thick black outlines. Flat colors only. Typography: uppercase, bold, blocky — like Bauhaus exhibition posters. Numbers are oversized (120pt+). The transition from chaos to harmony should be visually dramatic even in pure geometric abstraction. Inspired by El Lissitzky's "Beat the Whites with the Red Wedge" and Bauhaus pedagogical diagrams.

### Layout Concept

16:9 horizontal. Three equal panels separated by thick black vertical lines. Each panel has its own dominant background color (white / black / yellow), creating a strong triptych effect. The geometric shapes are the same basic set (triangles, squares, circles) but their arrangement tells the story — random → organizing → harmonious. Bottom 12%: full-width red CTA banner with bold white text. No rounded corners anywhere. Overall feel: a propaganda poster for rational procurement — bold, geometric, impossible to scroll past without reading.

### Format

16:9 horizontal — website conversion section / landing page

### Tone

Bold and declarative. "The choice is obvious." No soft persuasion — pure visual logic. Chaos vs. order. The geometric story makes the argument without words. Like a Bauhaus master explaining why grid-based design is superior to decoration — through demonstration, not explanation.
