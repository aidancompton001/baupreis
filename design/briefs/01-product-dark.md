# BauPreis AI — Product Infographic (Dark Tech Style)

## Creative Brief for Luma Labs

### Topic & Scope

A product explainer infographic showing how BauPreis AI works — from raw data collection to actionable insights. The visual tells the story of construction material prices flowing through an intelligent pipeline: data sources feed into AI analysis, which outputs forecasts, alerts, and cost optimization for German construction companies. No technical jargon — the audience should instantly understand the value.

### One Big Takeaway

Construction material prices change daily — BauPreis AI monitors 16 materials in real-time so you never overpay again. One platform replaces manual price tracking, spreadsheets, and guesswork.

### Target Audience

German construction professionals seeing BauPreis AI for the first time: Einkäufer (procurement managers), Bauleiter (site managers), Projektleiter (project managers). They are busy, skeptical of tech promises, and want to see concrete value in under 10 seconds.

### Narrative Flow

A left-to-right data pipeline story:

1. **DATA IN** — Icons of raw material sources: steel beams, concrete bags, copper pipes, lumber. Connected to API feeds (Eurostat, metals.dev, SMARD.de). Label: "16 Baumaterialien, Echtzeitdaten"

2. **AI ENGINE** — Central processing hub with neural network / brain visualization. Claude AI analyzes 3,000+ price data points. Label: "KI-Analyse & Prognose"

3. **INSIGHTS OUT** — Three output streams fanning out:
   - Price trend charts (line graphs going up/down)
   - Alert bell icons (instant notifications)
   - BauPreis Index gauge (market health indicator)

4. **YOUR BENEFIT** — End result: Euro sign with downward arrow = cost savings. Label: "Fundierte Einkaufsentscheidungen durch Echtzeitdaten"

### Key Stats to Include

- 16 construction materials monitored in real-time
- 3,000+ price data points analyzed
- AI forecasts updated daily via 7 automated jobs
- 3 plans starting at EUR 49/month
- Source: BauPreis AI platform data, 2026

### Visual Style

Dark, premium tech aesthetic. Deep navy background (#0A1528) with steel blue (#4A5C7E) secondary elements. White (#FFFFFF) text and labels for contrast. Orange accent for CTAs and key numbers. Lime green (#BEDD72) for positive indicators and growth arrows. Subtle grid lines resembling a trading terminal or Bloomberg dashboard. Metallic textures on material icons (steel reflection, concrete grain). Glowing data flow lines connecting pipeline stages. Modern sans-serif typography — clean, authoritative.

### Layout Concept

16:9 horizontal. Left-to-right flow with three main zones connected by glowing data stream lines. Left zone: scattered material icons converging into a funnel. Center zone: large AI brain/processor visual with radiating analysis waves. Right zone: three output cards (Trends, Alerts, Index) with sample data. Bottom bar: key stats in pill-shaped badges. Top-left: BauPreis AI logo. Overall feel: a premium fintech dashboard explaining itself.

### Format

16:9 horizontal — website hero section / landing page block

### Tone

Authoritative and premium. "This is serious technology for serious professionals." Confident, data-driven, trustworthy — like a Bloomberg terminal meets a modern SaaS landing page. No playfulness, no cartoons.
