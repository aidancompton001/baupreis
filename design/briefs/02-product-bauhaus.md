# BauPreis AI — Product Infographic (Bauhaus Style)

## Creative Brief for Luma Labs

### Topic & Scope

A product explainer infographic showing how BauPreis AI works — from raw data collection to actionable insights. The visual tells the story of construction material prices flowing through an intelligent pipeline: data sources feed into AI analysis, which outputs forecasts, alerts, and cost optimization for German construction companies. Bold geometric design language inspired by German Bauhaus tradition.

### One Big Takeaway

Construction material prices change daily — BauPreis AI monitors 16 materials in real-time so you never overpay again. One platform replaces manual price tracking, spreadsheets, and guesswork.

### Target Audience

German construction professionals seeing BauPreis AI for the first time: Einkäufer (procurement managers), Bauleiter (site managers), Projektleiter (project managers). They are busy, skeptical of tech promises, and want to see concrete value in under 10 seconds.

### Narrative Flow

A left-to-right data pipeline story using bold geometric shapes:

1. **DATA IN** — Geometric icons of raw materials: red square = steel, yellow triangle = concrete, black circle = copper. Each material is a distinct Bauhaus shape. Connected by thick black lines to the center. Label: "16 Baumaterialien, Echtzeitdaten"

2. **AI ENGINE** — Large red gear/cog at the center, interlocking with smaller gears. Inside: abstract geometric brain pattern. Label: "KI-Analyse & Prognose"

3. **INSIGHTS OUT** — Three bold output blocks:
   - Red rectangle: price trend bar chart
   - Yellow rectangle: alert notification icon
   - Black rectangle: BauPreis Index number

4. **YOUR BENEFIT** — Large yellow Euro symbol with bold downward arrow. Label: "Fundierte Einkaufsentscheidungen durch Echtzeitdaten"

### Key Stats to Include

- 16 construction materials monitored in real-time
- 3,000+ price data points analyzed
- AI forecasts updated daily via 7 automated jobs
- 3 plans starting at EUR 49/month
- Source: BauPreis AI platform data, 2026

### Visual Style

Bold German Bauhaus aesthetic. Primary palette: red (#C1292E), black (#1A1A1A), golden yellow (#F5C518), white (#FFFFFF), with terracotta (#BC8279) as warm accent. Strong geometric shapes — circles, triangles, rectangles — each representing a concept. Thick black outlines, no gradients, no shadows. Flat illustration style with construction iconography: cranes, buildings, gears, I-beams. Typography: bold, blocky sans-serif — impactful headlines. Grid-based composition with clear visual hierarchy. Inspired by Herbert Bayer, Moholy-Nagy, and modern geometric poster design.

### Layout Concept

16:9 horizontal. Modular grid layout divided into 4 bold blocks, left to right. Each block is a distinct color field (red, white, yellow, black) containing one pipeline stage. Blocks connected by thick geometric arrows. Material icons use simple Bauhaus shapes. Center block is largest (AI engine). Bottom strip: key stats in bold monospace type on black background. Top-left: BauPreis AI logo in Bauhaus variant. Overall feel: a German industrial design poster that also explains a SaaS product.

### Format

16:9 horizontal — website hero section / landing page block

### Tone

Bold, confident, unmistakably German. "Precision engineering meets digital innovation." Industrial strength, geometric clarity, Bauhaus heritage. Proud, modern, memorable — like a construction trade fair exhibit designed by a Bauhaus master.
