# BauPreis AI — Pain to Solution Marketing (Dark Tech Style)

## Creative Brief for Luma Labs

### Topic & Scope

A marketing conversion infographic that tells the classic "pain → solution → result" story. The viewer should feel their current pain (manual price tracking, surprise cost overruns, blind procurement), see BauPreis AI as the clear solution, and visualize the result (cost savings, time saved, confidence). This is the "closer" infographic — designed to convert a curious visitor into a trial signup.

### One Big Takeaway

Stop losing money to material price surprises. BauPreis AI gives you the same real-time price intelligence that large corporations have — starting at just EUR 49/month.

### Target Audience

Cold leads at the decision stage: Geschaeftsfuehrer (CEOs), CFOs, Einkaufsleiter (procurement heads) of small-to-medium German construction companies (5-200 employees). They control budgets, feel price pain personally, and can authorize a subscription today.

### Narrative Flow

A three-act story flowing left to right:

**ACT 1 — THE PAIN (left third, red/dark tones)**
- Visual: stressed construction professional at desk, surrounded by:
  - Scattered spreadsheets and price lists
  - Phone showing "Lieferant: Preis gestiegen +12%"
  - Red warning indicators everywhere
- Stats: "Mehrheit der Bauprojekte überschreitet das Materialbudget"
- Headline: "SO SIEHT IHR ALLTAG AUS"

**ACT 2 — THE SOLUTION (center third, glowing/transition)**
- Visual: BauPreis AI interface emerging from the chaos like a beacon
  - Clean dashboard replacing scattered papers
  - AI brain icon processing data
  - Alert bell with green checkmark
- Arrow/transition effect: chaos transforms into order
- Headline: "BAUPREIS AI ÜBERNIMMT"

**ACT 3 — THE RESULT (right third, green/positive tones)**
- Visual: confident professional with:
  - Clean dashboard on screen showing green trends
  - Time saved: clock showing "3 Stunden/Woche gespart"
  - Smart decisions: "Fundierte Einkaufsentscheidungen"
  - Peace of mind: shield with checkmark
- Headline: "SO KÖNNTE IHR ALLTAG AUSSEHEN"

**BOTTOM CTA** — "7 Tage kostenlos testen — baupreis.ais152.com"

### Key Stats to Include

- Material cost overruns affect the majority of construction projects (Source: Hauptverband der Deutschen Bauindustrie)
- Average procurement manager spends 3+ hours/week on manual price research
- BauPreis AI enables data-driven procurement decisions through real-time price intelligence
- 7-day free trial, no credit card required
- Starting at EUR 49/month (Basis plan)

### Visual Style

Cinematic dark tech with emotional storytelling. Left section: dark and chaotic — deep navy (#0A1528) with red warning glows and scattered elements creating visual stress. Center section: transitional — dark to light gradient, BauPreis AI interface glowing with steel blue (#4A5C7E) and orange accents, like a light source in darkness. Right section: resolved and calm — dark background with lime green (#BEDD72) positive indicators, clean lines, breathing space. The progression from chaos to clarity should be visually dramatic. Think: movie poster triptych. Typography: bold headlines, clean stats. Subtle particle effects and data flow lines connecting the three acts.

### Layout Concept

16:9 horizontal. Classic triptych layout — three equal-width columns with seamless visual flow between them. Left column is visually dense and chaotic (stacked, overlapping elements). Center column features the BauPreis AI logo/dashboard as a glowing focal point. Right column is clean and spacious (organized, aligned elements). A subtle gradient flows across all three, transitioning from dark-red to dark-blue to dark-green. Bottom 10%: full-width CTA bar with trial offer. Overall feel: a cinematic transformation story — "before, during, after" — told in one powerful horizontal frame.

### Format

16:9 horizontal — website conversion section / landing page above-the-fold

### Tone

Empathetic and compelling. "We understand your pain — here's your way out." Starts with tension (we feel your frustration), transitions through hope (technology to the rescue), ends with confidence (this is your new reality). Not aggressive sales — more like a trusted advisor showing you a better way.
