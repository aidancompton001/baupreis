# BauPreis AI Dashboard — Infographic (Bauhaus Style)

## Creative Brief for Luma Labs

### Topic & Scope

A stylized dashboard showcase infographic in Bauhaus geometric style — an artistic reinterpretation of the BauPreis AI dashboard. Instead of a realistic screenshot, the dashboard elements are translated into bold geometric compositions: charts become bar blocks, alerts become signal shapes, the index becomes a dial. The goal is to communicate dashboard functionality through striking Bauhaus design language.

### One Big Takeaway

Everything you need to control construction material costs — in one intelligent dashboard. Real-time prices, AI forecasts, smart alerts, and market indices, reimagined through the lens of German design heritage.

### Target Audience

Trial users exploring the platform and existing customers. This version appeals to design-conscious professionals who appreciate German craft tradition meeting modern technology. It communicates "this is a thoughtfully designed tool."

### Narrative Flow

A modular Bauhaus composition representing dashboard features:

1. **PRICE MONITORING** — Large geometric bar chart in the top-left quadrant. Vertical red (#C1292E) and yellow (#F5C518) bars of varying heights on black background. Each bar = a material. Bold label: "16 MATERIALIEN IN ECHTZEIT"

2. **AI FORECAST** — Top-right quadrant: abstract geometric pattern showing an upward trend — stacked triangles forming an ascending arrow. Inside: "KI-PROGNOSE +4.2%" in bold white type on red background

3. **SMART ALERTS** — Bottom-left quadrant: large yellow circle (alarm bell abstracted into a geometric shape) with concentric rings suggesting notification waves. Label: "PREISALARME — EMAIL, TELEGRAM, DASHBOARD"

4. **BAUPREIS INDEX** — Bottom-right quadrant: semicircular gauge made of geometric segments in red, yellow, terracotta. Needle pointing to "127.3". Label: "BAUPREIS INDEX"

5. **CENTER CONNECTOR** — Where all four quadrants meet: BauPreis AI logo in a circle, connecting all features

6. **BOTTOM STRIP** — Black bar with yellow text: "BASIS EUR 49 | PRO EUR 149 | TEAM EUR 299 — JETZT TESTEN"

### Key Stats to Include

- 16 materials tracked across the dashboard
- AI forecasts with 30/60/90 day horizons
- Real-time alerts via Email, Dashboard, Telegram
- BauPreis Index — proprietary market health indicator
- 3 plans for every company size

### Visual Style

Bold Bauhaus geometric interpretation. Red (#C1292E), black (#1A1A1A), golden yellow (#F5C518), white (#FFFFFF), terracotta (#BC8279). Each dashboard feature is abstracted into pure geometric shapes — no realistic UI elements. Charts are bold color blocks, not thin lines. Icons are geometric symbols (circle = alert, triangle = growth, square = stability). Thick black dividers between quadrants. Typography: uppercase bold sans-serif, oversized numbers. No gradients, no shadows, no rounded corners. Pure Bauhaus grid composition. Inspired by Kandinsky's "Composition" series and Bauhaus teaching diagrams.

### Layout Concept

16:9 horizontal. Strict 2x2 grid with thick black divider lines (like a Mondrian painting but with Bauhaus colors). Each quadrant contains one dashboard feature, abstracted geometrically. Center intersection: BauPreis AI logo circle. Bottom strip: pricing bar. No perspective, no 3D — purely flat, poster-like. Each quadrant has its own background color (alternating red/white/yellow/black). Overall feel: a Bauhaus teaching poster that explains a SaaS dashboard through pure geometric abstraction.

### Format

16:9 horizontal — website features section / product tour page

### Tone

Artistic and bold. "German engineering, German design." The visual statement is: we build tools with the same precision and aesthetic rigor as the Bauhaus masters designed buildings. Confident, geometric, unforgettable.
