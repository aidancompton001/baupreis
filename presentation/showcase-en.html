Procurement Tool

Price Escalation Calculator

Automatically calculate price adjustments for construction contracts using the official German Preisgleitklausel formula, fully compliant with VHB Form 225.

  • VHB Form 225 compliant — accepted by all German public procurement bodies
  • Formula: P = Base × (f + Σ(si × currenti / basei)) with configurable material cost shares
  • Base amount to adjusted amount calculated instantly with full transparency
